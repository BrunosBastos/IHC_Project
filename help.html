How to register

On the first page, Click on the Register button on top rigth.

The page will scroll you down to an area that gives you 2 options to register. Choose the one on the left if you are looking for a job or the one on the right if you want to publish your offers.

After that fill the input boxes with your information and sign in. If something is wrong the page will tell you what you need to do.
